Welcome To Altitude Charter

Altitude charter offers 37 years of charter experience. We have procured everything from 6-passenger turboprops to 300-passenger jumbo jets and everything in between. We not only know the best operators in the industry but also several of the individual aircraft.

We Listen and Learn

Once you fly with us, we’ll meticulously document your preferences, including aircraft size and type, as well as any food allergies your family may have for catering.

This personalized approach ensures that each subsequent booking is seamless and effortlessly tailored to your needs.
